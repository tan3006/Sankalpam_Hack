NSP Scholarship

National Scholarships Portal is one-stop solution through which various services starting from student application, application receipt, processing, sanction and disbursal of various scholarships to Students are enabled. National Scholarships Portal is taken as Mission Mode Project under National e-Governance Plan (NeGP)

National

Telangana state Scholarship

Online Pre Matric , Post Matric Scholarships disbursements through ePass application is one of the important welfare measures of the Government of Telangana which is aimed at the educational upliftment of Students, initiated by the Department of Backward Class Welfare. The system links all welfare departments, treasury, Colleges and Banks to disburse scholarships

JN TATA Scholarship

The JN Tata Endowment Scholarship is one of the most popular scholarship programs. This government scholarship for students is applicable if you are seeking higher education in international universities across the world.

National

ViidyaDhan Scholarship

The Vidyadhan Scholarship is a scholarship that is provided by the Sarojini Damodaran Foundation. It aims to provide financial support to economically weak and meritorious students to pursue higher education. Students are eligible to apply for the scholarship after they complete their class 10 board exams or equivalent. Currently, the scholarship can be availed across 8 states in India.

TS CM Overseas Scholarship 2023 is an Overseas Study Scheme was launched by the Telangana State Government for Minority students under the TS Foreign Education Scheme for minority students for pursuing higher studies in postgraduate in Foreign Universities.

National

Central Minority Scholarship

The Ministry of Minority Affairs was carved out of Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment and created on 29th January, 2006 to ensure a more focused approach towards issues relating to the notified minority communities namely Muslim, Christian, Budhist, Sikhs, Parsis and Jain. The mandate of the Ministry includes formulation of overall policy and planning, coordination, evaluation and review of the regulatory framework and development programmes for the benefit of the minority communities.

Minority E-Scholarship

National Scholarships Portal is one-stop solution through which various services starting from student application, application receipt, processing, sanction and disbursal of various scholarships to Students are enabled. National Scholarships Portal is taken as Mission Mode Project under National e-Governance Plan (NeGP)

Swami Vivekananda Minority Scholarship

The higher authorities of the Department of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of Gujarat support the students financially for technical and professional education like, diploma, BE, ME and BBA, MBA etc. The objective to introduce the scholarship is for the economic welfare and empowerment of minority students.
